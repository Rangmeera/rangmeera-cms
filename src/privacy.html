Privacy Policy

At RangMeera, we value your trust and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable laws in India.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Identity Data: Name, billing address, shipping address, email address, and phone number.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments to and from you. We do not store your credit card or UPI PIN details on our servers; these are securely processed by our authorized payment gateway partners.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to fulfill your orders, provide customer support, arrange for shipping via our courier partners, and send order status updates.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is only shared with trusted third parties necessary to run our business, such as payment gateways and logistics/courier companies, who are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at contact@rangmeera.in.
