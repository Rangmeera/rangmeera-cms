Shipping & Delivery Policy

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your RangMeera jewelry reaches you safely and securely.

1. Order Processing Time

All orders are processed and dispatched within 2 to 3 business days. Please note that we do not dispatch orders on Sundays or public holidays.

2. Estimated Delivery Time

Once dispatched, standard delivery usually takes 4 to 7 business days depending on your location within India. Delivery to remote areas may take slightly longer.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. Any specific promotional free shipping offers will be automatically applied at checkout.

4. Order Tracking

As soon as your package is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS so you can monitor your delivery status in real-time.
