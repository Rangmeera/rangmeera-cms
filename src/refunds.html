Cancellation & Refund Policy

At RangMeera, every piece of jewelry is carefully crafted and thoroughly inspected before dispatch. Due to the delicate nature of artificial ornaments and strict hygiene standards, we have a firm return policy.

1. Returns & Exchanges

For hygiene reasons—especially concerning earrings and necklace sets—we do not accept returns or exchanges simply for a change of mind. Returns are only accepted if the product received is fundamentally defective or damaged in transit.

2. The Unboxing Video Requirement (Mandatory)

To claim a replacement or refund for a damaged item, you must provide a continuous, unedited unboxing video.

  • The video must start with the sealed package showing the shipping label.
  • The defect must be clearly visible in the video.
  • Claims must be raised within 48 hours of delivery. Without a valid unboxing video, no claims for damage or missing items will be entertained.

3. Cancellations

You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided it has not already been dispatched. Once an order is handed over to our courier partners, it cannot be cancelled.

4. Refund Process

If your return is approved due to a transit defect, we will initiate a replacement. If the item is out of stock, a refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
